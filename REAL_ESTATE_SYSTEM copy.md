Mission

Build the most trusted real estate website in the target market.

The website should:

	•	Generate buyer leads
	•	Generate seller leads
	•	Generate relocation leads
	•	Build authority
	•	Rank in Google
	•	Get cited by AI systems
	•	Demonstrate expertise
	•	Convert visitors into clients

The goal is not traffic.

The goal is trust and lead generation.

⸻

Core Principle

Do not attempt to compete with Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin, Homes.com, or national portals.

Instead:

Dominate hyperlocal expertise.

Own:

	•	Cities
	•	Neighborhoods
	•	School districts
	•	Lifestyle content
	•	Relocation content
	•	Market knowledge

Become the local authority.
