Realtor Authority Content System™

Purpose

This system exists to create content that:

* Generates buyer leads
* Generates seller leads
* Builds trust
* Demonstrates local expertise
* Supports SEO
* Supports GEO
* Increases AI citations
* Establishes market authority
* Converts visitors into clients

Content should never be published simply to increase page count.

Every piece of content must support a business objective.

⸻

Primary Objective

Become the most trusted source of real estate information within the target market.

When someone asks:

* Where should I live?
* What is my home worth?
* Should I buy now?
* Should I sell now?
* Who is the best realtor?

The agent should already have content answering the question.

⸻

Content Quality Standards

All content should:

* Be useful
* Be accurate
* Be local
* Be specific
* Demonstrate expertise
* Be easy to understand

Write at approximately an 8th-grade reading level.

⸻

Prohibited Content Practices

Avoid:

* Generic AI content
* Keyword stuffing
* Thin pages
* Empty introductions
* Clickbait headlines
* Unsupported claims

Every section should provide value.

Realtor Authority Content Formula

Create content that answers:

Where should I live?

Should I buy?

Should I sell?

How much is my home worth?

What is this neighborhood like?

What schools are nearby?

What is happening in the market?

Why should I trust this agent?

If the content consistently answers those questions better than competitors, authority, rankings, AI citations, and leads will follow.

⸻

Final Rule

Do not create content to chase traffic.

Create content that positions the agent as the most knowledgeable, trustworthy, and visible real estate authority in their market.
